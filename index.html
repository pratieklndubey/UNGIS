Who We Are

Coordinating Global
Digital Cooperation

The United Nations Group on the Information Society (UNGIS) constitutes the comprehensive inter-agency coordination mechanism for the implementation of the WSIS outcomes within the UN System.

We work to bridge the digital divide and ensure that the benefits of new technologies are available to all, aligning digital advancements with the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

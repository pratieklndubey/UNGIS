About UNGIS

The United Nations Group on the Information Society (UNGIS) is the principal inter-agency mechanism for coordinating the implementation of the World Summit on the Information Society (WSIS) outcomes within the UN system.

Establishing a productive synergy between 32 UN agencies, UNGIS works to bridge the digital divide, strengthen capacities, and align digital strategy with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

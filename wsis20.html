Process & Review

WSIS+20 Review

20 Years of the implementation of the WSIS Process. UNGIS coordinates the collective inputs of its member agencies to ensure the continued relevance and impact of the Information Society vision.

The Mandate

UNGIS supports UN system’s efforts to help bridge digital divides, strengthen capacities, and align digital transformation efforts with the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It contributes significantly to the annual WSIS Forum.
